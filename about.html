The HuntArts Origin

I'm the engine: one guy, one laptop, endless grind out of Mbarara, Uganda. No agency bullshit, no investors, no excuses — just raw creativity crashing into software to solve real problems for mid-level businesses here and beyond.

Mbarara streets raised this: boda-bodas dodging chaos, markets buzzing 24/7, clock tower watching the hustle. That energy? It's in every bot flow I build, every site I launch, every edit I drop. We craft sites that load on Ugandan networks and convert. We edit videos/photos until they hit different. And yeah — collabs are free if the vibe's right, training negotiable if you're ready to learn the hunt.
